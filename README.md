# Weird Web Experiments

These are small internet side experiments that explore culture, branding and the psychology of attention.

## This Domain Sucks
A site where founders submit their questionable domain names and get roasted.
https://thisdomain.sucks

why this matters
• founders get out of their bubble
• the roast goes viral
• the internet gets a laugh
• everyone learns what makes a domain memorable

## imThinking
This is a place for strange ideas to be published like small micro blog thoughts.
https://imthinking.com

why this matters
• people want permission to think out loud
• micro content is the future of longform
• the internet needs more experimental thinking
